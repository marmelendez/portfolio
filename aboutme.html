About me

Hi my name is Maribel Melendez. I’m currently studying my third year of B.S. in Computer Science and Technology at Tecnológico de Monterrey in Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico. I'm passionate about my career, and I'm also interested in design and digital art. Not too much time ago, I wrote my first hello world! program, since that I decided to continue learning more about programming and I have been enjoying this path.

I like learning new things, learn from others and also from my mistakes. I love how technology is a great tool that help us make our life easier, and I want to be on the side that contributes to provide part of this technology to the rest too. For me, technology always comes with new challenges and the part I most enjoy about this is the analysis and creativity that is required to face them.
